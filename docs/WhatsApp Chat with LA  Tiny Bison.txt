4/5/26, 1:25 PM - Steve Lozano: Clock in / time cards

What we need: Hours assigned to project + service item (for bonus calc and QBO sync)
What workers will actually do: As little data entry as possible

Three UX Options

Option A: "Daily Digest" (Recommended)

Flow:

1. Worker geo-clocks in at site (passive, no action)
2. System auto-detects: "You're at 215 Cinnamon Teal"
3. End of day: Push notification → "Confirm today's work"
4. Screen shows:
  • Project: [The District] ✓ (auto-filled from geo)
  • Service items worked: [Basecoat, Finish Coat] (pre-filled from project scope)
  • Hours: [8] (auto-calculated from clock-in/out)
Worker just taps Confirm or edits if wrong.

Why it works: One tap per day. Zero cognitive load. Crew lead can do it for whole team.

───

Option B: "Scope Checkpoints"

Flow:

1. Worker starts day, sees: "Working on The District?"
2. Taps Yes → auto-assigned
3. During day, quick-action buttons for scope items: "Switching to Flashing"
4. End of day: Review and confirm

Trade-off: More granular data (good for production tracking), but requires more interaction.

───

Option C: "Foreman Bulk Mode"

Flow:

1. Crew lead has tablet/phone at end of day
2. Sees whole crew's geo-clock times
3. Drag-drop or quick-assign each worker to projects + service items
4. One submission covers 4-6 people


Or can do a hybrid. For example a mix of A and C
